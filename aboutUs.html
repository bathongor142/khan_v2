Introduction

Currently “Khaanzaa Service” LLC are operating for 6 different industrial fields. In accordance with the special license granted by the Water Service Regulatory Commission of Mongolia, our company adheres to international standards to provide a wide range of services. These services encompass the operation and maintenance of wastewater treatment facilities, testing and adjustment of valve boxes and sewage line assemblies, the supply and installation of water and sewerage equipment, and the transportation of wastewater using specially designed vehicles. In accordance with the special license granted by the Ministry of Construction and Urban Development of Mongolia, which authorizes the construction of buildings and the development of water and wastewater treatment facilities, we offer a comprehensive suite of services encompassing design, construction,
supply, and installation of diverse products across multiple industry We have fulfilled all the special licences for the all sectors of our services which are confirmed by government and state organizations. There are 5 different types of State Special licences and 2 local permits which are regarding our services. Today our team has 300 all national members and 2 foreign consultants. Every year, we have achieved our goal to increase the total percentage of our local employees for OT Project. It is our mission to introduce international standards through our activities and construction and to improve our social responsibility by providing discipline and quality services. As a core part of achieving our goal, we apply the ISO 9001:2015, ISO 14001:2015, and ISO 45001:2018 standards in our operation.
